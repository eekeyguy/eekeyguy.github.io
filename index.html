The Magician

The Magician traditionally symbolizes creation, and the energy or will to create. For the Magician, knowledge is the source of skill and ability – like Prometheus in Greek legend, he seeks to capture and channel the power of nature to conjure new possibilities in the world.

CONCEPT & PRACTICE

Inspired by the fine-art tarot collections of Salvador Dalì and Andrea Picini, each piece starts life as a pencil or ink sketch which is scanned, recolored, and digitally manipulated to further randomize both aesthetic and effect. My aim is to balance conscious creative intention with a considered relinquishing of control, increasing the ambiguity of line and form, and making space for unexpected emotional or narrative interpretations to proliferate.

Siren's Kiss

In this captivating image, we witness the tantalizing beauty of a woman's lips, glistening with droplets of water, as if tempting us to take a sip from this mystical wellspring. And yet, just inches away from this alluring vision, a snake looms, poised with a sense of purpose, its sinuous body tracing a graceful curve downwards. This juxtaposition of danger and desire, of temptation and trepidation, creates a sense of tension and intrigue that draws us in, inviting us to explore the deeper mysteries hidden within this enigmatic image.
